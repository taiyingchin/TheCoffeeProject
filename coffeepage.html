Our Coffee

Want gourmet quality coffee at home? We partner with roasters from the local community to deliver you the freshest coffee you've come to know and love.

Dogwood Coffee

Founded in 2010, Dogwood Coffee strives to bring coffee to the people. With the motto of "Coffee For Others", Dogwood makes it their mission to provide coffee for others through sustainable practices and mutual commitment to the community and themselves in order to sustain jobs, opportunities, and our greater lives.

Spyhouse Coffee

Spyhouse is a Twin Cities based roaster and retailer that cultivates relationships with those who share passion and vision to source, roast, and serve exceptional coffees. Spyhouse works towards building strong producer partnerships that create sustainable practices in order to combat challenges that climate change brings to the coffee community.

Five Watt

Five Watt Coffee was founded by Lee Carter and Caleb Garn in 2014. Five Watt Coffee strives to bring the best coffee experience to people in the Twin Cities through excellent customer service and partnerships with roasters.
